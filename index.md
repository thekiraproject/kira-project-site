# The Kira Project

Kira is a local-first compound-AI system: one 9-billion-parameter model serving
every pipeline role — router, specialists, synthesizer, agentic planner — with
~50 live tools, on a single Apple Silicon machine, with zero cloud inference.
Built and operated by one person working with AI pair-engineering, governed by
architecture decision records, and verified by a fixture of 1,100+ live
assertions that mechanically define correct behavior.

**This site publishes what we measure.**
